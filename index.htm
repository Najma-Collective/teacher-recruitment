At Al Manar Society: Noor, we deliver a range of ELT-centred online programmes,

working with Palestinians across the West Bank, Gaza and Egypt.

For our students, education is life, preserving collective memory and sustaining community in the face of erasure.

Our core aim is to develop a life-giving online curriculum

grounded in exploratory learning and decolonial praxis.

We centre our work on developing capacity,

from building psychological resilience

through to practical skills training.

In this way, our programmes directly serve

the immediate and long-term needs

of the communities we engage.

We’re looking for volunteer

General English/IELTS teachers

and ELT materials designers

who want to…

  • make a real and measurable difference in the lives of Palestinian students
  • gain experience in a generative educational space, where creativity and innovation are celebrated
  • become part of an international network of teachers brought together by a shared belief in the liberatory potential of education
  • participate in a grassroots educational movement rooted in sumud

If all this resonates, we’d love to

welcome you to our community.
